HackerNews Follow Discussion Button

Welcome to the first version of the HackerNews Follow Discussion Button.

Basically, this is a plain vanilla link button, without any counter or anything. Just a .html and .css file.

What can the Button be used for?

It could be a nice way for young startups or upcoming YC batches to insert a small button, linking to their website-related discussion on HackerNews (news.ycombinator.com). This button is a different approach, compared to the HN Like Button. It´s different, as it only serves as a link to the discussion, without any direct influence on points.

Example Use

Just change the link in the .html code "http://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=XXXXXX" by replacing XXXXXX with your link id . For example, you could insert the button into your footer or community section. Why not.
